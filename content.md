## Code of Ethics

It is the policy of Vectra AI, Inc. to conduct its affairs in strict compliance with the letter and spirit of the law and to adhere to the highest principles of business ethics.

**Key Points:**
- Avoid activities in conflict with company interests
- A conflict of interest exists when loyalties or actions are divided between Company's interests and another party (competitor, supplier, customer)
- Both the fact and appearance of conflict should be avoided
- Report uncertain situations to parties named in the Reporting Violations section

**Examples of situations to avoid:**
1. Revealing or misusing confidential information
2. Accepting personal gifts, entertainment, favors, or payments from competitors, customers, suppliers, or potential suppliers
3. Participating in organizations that might involve divulging confidential company information
4. Developing personal relationships with subordinates or employees of competitors/suppliers/customers that might interfere with impartial judgment

**Disclosure Requirements:**
- If you or someone with a close relationship has a financial or employment relationship with a competitor, customer, supplier, or potential supplier: disclose in writing to Human Resources
- Personal relationships with subordinates or competitor/supplier/customer employees may require full disclosure
